Benefits of NSQ

To Company

  1. NSQ assure quality and performance – links national standards to operations of companies
  2. Helps you aim for quality awards
  3. Gives greater confidence in management
  4. Demonstrates compliance with legislation
  5. Increases productivity and efficiency
  6. Help retain staff longer – saving on recruitment costs
  7. Cost effective way to train staff
  8. Increases profits
  9. Aid to writing contracts
  10. Help employer to recruit people with the required competencies
  11. Provides proof that employee can work to industry standards in a particular job, role or disciple
  12. It shows customers that an employer has competent workforce who can provide quality product/service
  13. Can help to structure on-the-job training
  14. Can help to reduce accidents as NSQs cover health and safety practices on the job

To Individuals

  1. Installs confidence in their abilities
  2. Gives a sense of motivation and achievement
  3. Gives a recognised qualification for existing skills without having to attend College
  4. Provides evidence of competency in a skill base, which could attract promotion or greater responsibility
  5. Recognized by employers as measure of skills and abilities (competence)
  6. Allows him/her to be assessed at work while doing the job
  7. NSQ are completed at a pace to suit each individual
  8. The training is highly flexible, one can choose to do a full NSQ or just Units of it and get certified for the areas covered
  9. If she/he changes job, the NSQ can still be used on another job requiring the same competencies

To the Nation

  1. Improve the economy through well trained workforce
  2. Help to reduce unemployment
  3. Helps in providing trained workforce for export
  4. Provide quality products and services which are competitive in the market

Employees with NSQs are better motivated, more loyal, reliable and adaptable.
